Ordering

Please place your orders by 10:00 am. Orders can be placed for the current day only.

To order a meal, please click on the name of the meal. An Internet browser window will open showing details of your order. Please check the information and submit your order.

To order multiple meals, either use the links in this email multiple times or use the "Today's menu" and submit each meal order from within the Web application.

Delivery

The food will be available for pickup from 11:30 at Accenture reception.

Payment

Please place the money (or vouchers) into an envelope available next to the paper box labeled "Lunch4you". Please write your name on the sticker on the envelope (DON'T seal it, so that the envelope can be reused again) and place it in the paper box. In case you don't have exact change, some coins will be placed next to the box for your use.
