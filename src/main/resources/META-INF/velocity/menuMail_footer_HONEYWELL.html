Ordering

Please place your orders by 10:30 am. Orders can be placed for the current day only.

To order a meal, please click on the name of the meal. An Internet browser window will open showing details of your order. Please check the information and submit your order.

To order multiple meals, either use the links in this email multiple times or use the "Today's menu" link and submit each meal order from within the Web application.

Delivery

The food will be delivered every day at 12:00 to the Honeywell office. Food will be available for pickup at the reception - Building 18, 3rd floor.

Payment

Money / vouchers will be collected by your office management and handed over to Lunch4you at the arrival of your meals.
