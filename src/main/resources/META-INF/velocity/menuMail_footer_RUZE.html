Ordering

Please place your orders any time on the day of order at least 30 mintes before the pickup. Last orders will be accepted before 16:00. Orders can be placed for the current day only.

To order a meal, please click on the name of the meal. An Internet browser window will open showing details of your order. In the comments field please state the time when you want to pick up your meal at the restaurant. Please check the information and submit your order.

To order multiple meals, either use the links in this email multiple times or use the "Today's menu" and submit each meal order from within the Web application.

Pickup

The food will be available at the time stated in your order at the Lunch4you restaurant. Please click here for Lunch4you location detailed details.
