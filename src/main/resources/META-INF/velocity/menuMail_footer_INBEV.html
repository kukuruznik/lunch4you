Ordering

Please place your orders by 10:30 am. Orders can be placed for the current day only.

To order a meal, please click on the name of the meal. An Internet browser window will open showing details of your order. Please check the information and submit your order.

To order multiple meals, either use the links in this email multiple times or use the "Today's menu" and submit each meal order from within the Web application.

Delivery

The food will be delivered every day from 12:00 to the AB Inbev reception (The Park - Building 18) office. Food will be available for pickup at the reception.

Payment

Please place the money (or vouchers) into an envelope available in the kitchen and put it into the paper box labeled "Lunch4you". Please write your name on the sticker on the envelope (DON'T seal it, so that the envelope can be reused again). In case you don't have exact change, some coins will be placed next to the box for your use.
