Ordering

Please try to order before 10:30 am if possible. Orders will be accepted by 11:00 am latest.

To order a meal, please click on the name of the meal. An Internet browser window will open showing details of your order. Please check the information and submit your order.

To order multiple meals, either use the links in this email multiple times or use the "Today's menu" and submit each meal order from within the Web application.

Delivery

The food will be delivered every day by 12:00 am and will be available for pickup in front of the DHL Mail Room, Ground Floor, Building 7. Every meal that you order will be labeled with your name.

Payment

Please place the money (or vouchers) into an envelope available inside the Mail Room and put it into the paper box labeled "Lunch4you". Please write your name on the sticker on the envelope (DON'T seal it, so that the envelope canbe reused again). Some change coins are placed next to the box in case you don't have exact change.
